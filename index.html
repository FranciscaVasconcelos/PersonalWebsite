  ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Francisca Vasconcelos, a Portuguese-American, born in Boston and raised in San Diego. I attended Torrey Pines High School where, in my 10th grade AP Computer Science, I discovered my passion for programming. Since then, I have worked on a number of fun projects ranging from programming drones to follow people autonomously based on computer vision to developing a quantum phyiscs Android app to building a shape-shifting origami robot. I am currently a member of the MIT Class of 2020 and plan to major in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science, with a potential double major in Physics. I am interested in exploring quantum computing. I hope to some day become a researcher and help solve some of humanity's largest challenges through development of cutting-edge technology.
